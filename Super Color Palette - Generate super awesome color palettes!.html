Super Color Palette
generates super awesome color palettes

Made for artists, designers, and super awesome people.

Hue Shift
generates analogous colors

Change how much the hue shifts along the color wheel for each color in the palette.

Saturation Shift
generates greyscale

Distributes the amount of saturation equally throughout the entire color palette. Use it to fine-tune your color palette for the look you want.

Light Shift
generates color shades

Distributes the amount of lightness equally throughout the entire color palette. Perfect for monochromatic illustration styles or complete light-to-dark color palettes.

Groups
let you be super creative

Super Color Palettes can have up to 5 color groups and 20 colors per group for a maximum of 100 colors!

About

Hey, 👋 Thanks for checking out Super Color Palette! As a designer, I wanted an easy way to quickly generate color palettes that shift in hue, saturation, or lightness in equal amounts. So I made it! It's designed so that you can experiment and explore unique color combinations and easily share or download. I hope that Super Color Palette becomes a tool that you love to use as part of your creative process.
