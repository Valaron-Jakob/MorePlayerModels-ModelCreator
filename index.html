MorePlayerModels
Model Creator

by Valaron/Jakob

What is this?

This is a tool to convert models from Blockbench to the MorePlayerModels mod. It is not a replacement for Blockbench, but instead a tool to make the process of creating models easier.

I have created this tool to make the process of converting and configuring models for the mod easier and faster.


How To

  • Fill out the fields on the form page below according to your model.
  • Create a model you want to import with Blockbench and export it in the Bedrock format.
  • Copy all the content of the "model.geo.json" you exported into the model section on the form.
  • Click on "Download" to get your finished model in the MPM format.

Form

Input all the information about your model here and paste the Blockbench model inside the big textarea.

⚠ Hover over the labels to get more information about the input.
